Different Spirits

  • Apparitions: Full-bodied ghost sightings.
  • Poltergeists: Known for moving objects and causing disturbances.
  • Residual Hauntings: Energy repeating events like a recording.
  • Shadow People: Dark, human-like figures seen briefly.
